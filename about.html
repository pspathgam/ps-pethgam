About Our School

Government Primary School Pethgam Wagoora, located in the scenic area of Wagoora in District Baramulla, Jammu & Kashmir, is committed to providing high-quality foundational education. With a child-friendly environment, trained educators, and a focus on holistic development, the school has become a trusted institution for early learning in the community.

Our mission is to nurture young minds with discipline, creativity, and values that empower them to become confident and responsible citizens.

Our Mission

To deliver joyful, engaging, and inclusive education to every child, ensuring foundational literacy and numeracy, strong moral values, and equal learning opportunities for all.

Our Vision

To transform young learners into confident, curious, and capable individuals—ready to embrace challenges and succeed in higher education and life.
