About Government Primary School Pethgam Wagoora

A leading public institution dedicated to providing high-quality foundational education, nurturing character, and fostering a passion for lifelong learning in the community of Pethgam, Wagoora.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission: To create a safe, inclusive, and learning-focused environment where every child is empowered with essential literacy, numeracy, and life skills (as per FLN guidelines).

We are committed to providing quality education supported by dedicated teachers and essential resources, ensuring that our students grow into confident, creative, and compassionate citizens.

Our Vision: To be recognized as the cornerstone of primary education in the Wagoora area, delivering educational excellence that shapes resilient and socially responsible young minds ready for the next level of schooling.

School History & Identity

Established as a vital educational institution for the Pethgam community, our school has been serving students from Pre-Primary (Aanganwadi) to Grade 5. We operate under the guidance of the Zonal Education Office (ZEO) Wagoora and strictly adhere to state and national educational frameworks.

Academic Approach: Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN)

Our curriculum is centered on achieving the objectives of the **Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN)** mission for all our students in the primary stage.

We use activity-based learning, storytelling, and play-way methods to ensure learning is engaging and effective. Continuous evaluation and remedial classes are provided to ensure no child is left behind in achieving foundational competencies.

"Joyful learning through hands-on activities is the core of our educational philosophy."

Infrastructure and Facilities

Though a primary school, we strive to provide the best possible learning environment for our students. Our facilities include:

  • **Classrooms:** Three well-maintained and adequately lit classrooms.
  • **Library Corner:** A dedicated space with 350+ books to promote reading habits.
  • **Mid-Day Meal (MDM):** A dedicated kitchen and dining area ensuring hygienic and timely nutritious meals.
  • **Safety:** Separate washroom facilities for boys and girls, and a secured compound wall.
  • **Teaching Aids:** Availability of charts, globes, and digital learning content.
