Academic Overview

At Government Primary School Pethgam Wagoora, we follow a structured and child-centric curriculum aligned with the guidelines of the School Education Department, Jammu & Kashmir. Our academic plan ensures continuous learning, holistic development, and competency-based progress for all learners.

Curriculum Structure

Our curriculum includes core academic subjects, co-curricular activities, foundational learning, and regular assessments for grades Nursery to 5th.

Subjects Offered

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • EVS
  • Urdu
  • Kashmiri (as per requirement)
  • Art & Craft
  • Physical Education
  • Moral/Value Education
