Admissions Open for Session 2025-26

Give your child the best foundation with our focused curriculum and dedicated teaching staff. We welcome applications for Pre-Primary (Aanganwadi) and Class 1 to 5.

Step 1 Check Eligibility & Collect Documents

Ensure your child meets the age criteria for the desired grade level and prepare the necessary government documents.

Key Eligibility Criteria

Pre-Primary (Aanganwadi): Child must be 3+ years as of April 1st.
Class I: Child must be 5+ years as of April 1st.
Transfer Admission: Only possible if transferring from another Government or recognized Private school with a valid Transfer Certificate (TC).

Step 2 Visit the School Office for Registration

Visit the school office during the official admission window to complete the registration form and submit the required paperwork for verification.

  • **Required Documents:**
    • Parent/Guardian Aadhaar Card (Copy)
    • Child's Birth Certificate (Original + Copy for verification)
    • Child's Aadhaar Card (if available)
    • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school (for Class 2-5 admissions)
    • Latest Passport-size Photograph of the child (2 copies)

Step 3 Verification and Enrollment

The Head of Institution (HOI) will review the documents and confirm the seat availability. Enrollment will be completed after successful verification.

**No Fee is charged for admission or tuition** in Government Primary Schools (as per government policy). Parents are only required to ensure timely submission of documents.

Admission Window (Annual Session 2025-26)

The official admission period begins: **March 15th**

Please contact the Head of Institution directly during school hours (9:30 AM to 2:30 PM) for any urgent queries.
